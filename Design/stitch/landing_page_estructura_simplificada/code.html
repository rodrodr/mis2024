The Methodology of Democratic Persistence

Our analysis transcends simple electoral results. We utilize a multi-dimensional framework to assess how local governance absorbs national shocks and maintains institutional continuity. Through weighted algorithmic processing, we convert raw TSE (Tribunal Superior Eleitoral) data into a Decisive Record of democratic health.

Polarization: The Diverging Index

Polarization is not just disagreement; it is the spatial distance between competing ideologies within a municipal council. Our index tracks the "hollowing out" of the center-neutral ground over three decades.

  • Opposition / Left

    Radicalized dissent and labor-centric mobilization markers.

  • Center / Neutral

    Technocratic stability and coalition-building nodes.

  • Proponents / Right

    Institutional conservatism and market-liberal indicators.
